Sprint Calendar

This is our Sprint Calendar Page. Here we can dumb informaton about the timeline or the overarching goal for the Scrum Team.

  • 1 week sprints, starting Tuesday Morning at the start of the Sprint Planning meeting.
  • Sprint Ends on Monday at the end of the Sprint Retrospective.
  • Sprint Events are:
    • Daily Scrum - 15 minute time-box - Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday
      • what you're doing to accomplish the Sprint Goal,
      • what you did yesterday to accomplish the Sprint Goal,
      • are you blocked form accomplishing the Sprint Goal.
    • Sprint Planning - 1 hour time-box - Tuesday
      • Go over what the Sprint Goal is and identify the work that needs to get "Done".
    • Sprint Review - 30 minute time-box - Monday
      • Go over and demo what was/wasn't "Done" during the Sprint with key stakeholders.
    • Sprint Retrospective - 30 minute time-box - Monday
      • Go over what went weel, what didn't go well, and how do we improve on the process.
